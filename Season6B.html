Star Wars: The Clone Wars - Season 6B

When the Clone Wars was cancelled, half of Season 6 was finished, while the other half was not. Season 6B is the 3 story arcs meant for Season 6 that were left unfinished.

As the Rush Clovis Arc was initially planned for Season 5, there are two known season episode numbers for these story arcs.

Please click on a Image to go each arc's respective page:

522-525: The Bounty Hunter Arc (Season 6 Episode 11-14/14-17)

Starring all of the major Bounty Hunters but highly focused on Cad Bane and Boba Fett, this Story Arc would've cemented who was the best Bounty Hunter in the Galaxy.

601-604: Crystal Crisis on Utapau (Season 6 Episode 15-18/18-21)

In an Budy-Cop Style Adventure, Obi-Wan and Anakin discover a huge seperatist conspiracy.

605-608: Ahsoka's Walkabout (Season 6 Episode 19-22/22-25) 

 

Having left the Jedi Order, Ahsoka tries to find reasoning in the lower levels of Courscant. (This story arc was the first out of three to be finished in 2020 but was altered from its original version)
